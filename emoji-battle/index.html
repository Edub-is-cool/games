⚔️ Emoji Battle ⚔️

Build your base. Train your army. Destroy your enemy!

How to Play

Player 1 (Blue) — Left side
Player 2 (Red) — Right side

Players take turns. On your turn:

  • Click the Shop to buy buildings or soldiers
  • Click your side of the grid to place buildings
  • Soldiers auto-march toward the enemy base
  • Destroy the enemy 🏰 Castle to win!
